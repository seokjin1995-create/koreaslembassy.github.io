1. About the Embassy

The Embassy of the Republic of Korea in Sierra Leone represents the Government of the Republic of Korea and works to promote diplomatic, economic, and cultural relations between Korea and Sierra Leone. We are currently seeking qualified local staff to support the establishment and operation of the Embassy.

2. Position Overview

The Embassy is hiring local administrative and technical support staff as part of its opening preparations. Two positions are available: Administrative Secretary and Driver (Facility-Support).

3. Responsibilities

Administrative Secretary

  • Assist in general administrative and clerical tasks within the Embassy
  • Prepare official documents, correspondence, and reports in English
  • Conduct research on political, economic, and social issues related to Sierra Leone
  • Manage schedules, meetings, and appointments for Embassy staff
  • Provide translation and interpretation support (English–Krio) when necessary
  • Support Embassy events and communication activities

Driver (Facility-Support)

  • Safely operate Embassy vehicles for official duties
  • Perform basic vehicle maintenance and record-keeping
  • Assist with facility management and minor repairs (plumbing, electrical, etc.)
  • Support logistical tasks such as deliveries and event preparation

4. Qualifications

Administrative Secretary

  • Mandatory Requirements: Native or near-native English proficiency, Bachelor's degree or higher (Master's preferred), proficiency in office software (MS Office, Google Workspace)
  • Preferred Qualifications: Master’s degree or higher, research and analytical skills related to Sierra Leone and African affairs, previous administrative or embassy experience, interest in Korean culture and international cooperation
  • Key Competencies: Administrative and clerical support, research and data organization, communication, English–Krio translation, event coordination

Driver (Facility-Support)

  • Mandatory: Valid driver’s license and clean record, knowledge of Freetown roads, facility maintenance experience, basic English communication
  • Preferred: Experience at embassies or international organizations, technical certificates (mechanical/electrical)
